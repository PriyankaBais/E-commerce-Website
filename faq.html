Frequently Asked Questions



1. What is SnapNShop?

Ans. SnapNShop is an online marketplace where you can find amazing products across various categories at the best prices.


2. How can I track my order?

Ans. Once your order is placed, you can track it from the 'My Orders' section in your account.


3. What is the return policy?

Ans. You can return eligible items within 7 days of delivery. Check the product details for return eligibility.


4. How do I contact customer support?

Ans. You can reach out to our support team via the 'Support' section on our website.


5. Do you offer international shipping?

Ans. Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping charges may vary based on the destination.


6. What payment methods do you accept?

Ans. We accept major credit/debit cards, UPI, PayPal, and net banking.


7. How do I cancel my order?

Ans. You can cancel your order from the 'My Orders' section before it is shipped. If already shipped, contact support.


8. Are my payment details secure?

Ans. Yes, all payments are encrypted and processed through secure gateways to ensure maximum safety.


9. Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Ans. Yes, but only before the order is shipped. You can update your address in the 'My Orders' section.


10. Do you offer discounts or loyalty programs?

Ans. Yes! We offer seasonal discounts, promo codes, and a loyalty program where customers earn points for every purchase.
